Overflow Property

The overflow property determines how and when to display a scroll bar.

There are options for horizontal (overflow-x) and vertical (overflow-y) scolling as well.

Here are the options:

  • visible
    • overflow: visible; is the default and shows the content outside the element
  • hidden
    • overflow: hidden; will cut off any content that doesn't fit
  • scroll
    • overflow: scroll; shows a scroll bar
  • auto
    • overflow: auto; shows a scroll bar only when needed
